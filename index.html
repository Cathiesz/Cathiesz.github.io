Rules


This game has been inspired by the classic Snake. In this digging adventure you control the Meeple on his adventure to find as many treasure as possible.


Rules

Once the game has started u will see random squares poping up on your screen. By moving your Meeple you have to get to the X on your screen and press the Dig button to get a Treasure (Score points go yeeee). But attention. If you hit a Square the game will end. If you hit a Square some random Squares will disappear from your Map.


Movement PC

On the PC you can move your Meeple by using the arrow keys.


Movement Mobile

On Mobile you can move your Meeple by tilting your Phone.


How to dig?

Well this is the important part. You dig by screaming when you are near an X
