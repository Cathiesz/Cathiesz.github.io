Rules for "The Amazing Dig!"
The Amazing Dig! is a snake inspired Web-App Game that is playable on Mobile and PC
Rules

Once the game has started u will see random squares poping up on your screen. By moving your Meeple you have to get to the X on your screen and press the Dig button to get a Treasure (Score points go yeeee). But attention. If you hit a

Square

the game will end. If you hit a

Square

some random

Squares

will disappear from your Map.

Movement PC

On the PC you can move your Meeple by using the arrow keys.

Movement Mobile

On Mobile you can move your Meeple by tilting your Phone.
